The Weight

A story about what we did, and what it did to us

Daniel Voss is a man who knows exactly what he did twelve years ago. He has not, in all that time, told a single living person the truth. Now something has found him — and it will not leave until he decides what kind of man he intends to be.

⚠   Contains themes of grief, guilt, and moral injury
